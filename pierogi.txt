Pierogi with potatoes and cheese

Dough:
3 cups 245g all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 large egg
1/2 cup 112g sour cream
4 tablespoon 55g butter

Filling:
1 1/2 cup 339g mashed potatoes, warm
1 cup 111g white cheese, shredded


To finish:
4 tablespoon, 57g butter, cold
2 large 156g shallots, disced, or one medium 156g onion, sliced.
