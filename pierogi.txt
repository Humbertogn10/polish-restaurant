Pierogi with potatoes and cheese

Dough
2 cups 246g all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 large egg
1/2 cup 113g sour cream
4 tablespoon 57g butter

Filling
1 cup 227g mashed potatoes, warm
1 cup 112g sharp cheddar cheese, shredded

To finish
4 tablespoon, 57g butter, cold
2 large 156g shallots, disced, or one medium 156g onion, sliced.
